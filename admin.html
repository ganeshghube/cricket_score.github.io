Rules enforced: Wide & No-Ball = NOT a legal delivery — over count stays, extra ball bowled (over may have 7, 8… deliveries). No-Ball → FREE HIT next ball (shown on overlay). Byes & Leg Byes = legal delivery (count in over), runs go to extras only — NOT to batsman or bowler economy. Odd runs on any delivery → striker rotates. End of over (6 legal balls) → automatic strike swap + ball grid resets. Wicket on Free Hit → only run-out counts; wicket NOT credited if it's a FH (use Error Correction if needed).
